# 🕉️ Vedic AI Copilot: Supported Queries

This document provides a comprehensive list of all supported queries and intents that the Vedic AI Reasoning Engine can process. You can combine these queries (e.g., *"What is my Lagna and is today good for business?"*) or specify dates (e.g., *"Travel Muhurat tomorrow"*).

---

## 🌅 1. Personal Birth Profile
Get insights based on your birth details (Natal Chart).
- **Lagna (Ascendant):**
  - *"What is my Lagna?"*
  - *"Tell me my rising sign"*
  - *"Which sign rises at my birth?"*
- **Birth Nakshatra (Star):**
  - *"What is my Nakshatra?"*
  - *"Which star was I born in?"*
  - *"My birth Nakshatra and pada"*
- **Birth Chart (Kundali):**
  - *"Show my birth chart"*
  - *"Generate my Janma Kundali"*
  - *"My horoscope chart"*
